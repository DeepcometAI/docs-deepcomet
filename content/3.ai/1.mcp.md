## Introduction

The DeepComet Model Family includes specialized models for different layers of the stack.

## DeepComet-Zenith

10 Billion parameter model for kernel optimization, system health, and security.

## DeepComet-Prime

Trillion-parameter model for general intelligence tasks and system-wide coordination.

## DeepComet-Code

Specialized model for automatic migration of legacy codebases into Aurelia. Also known as "The Forge."

## DeepComet-Mobile

Optimized model for edge devices and mobile deployment.

## Model Family Overview

| Model | Parameters | Purpose |
