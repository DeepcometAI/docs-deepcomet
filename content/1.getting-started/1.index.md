## Key Principles

- **Vertical AI Integration**: Unlike competitors who run AI workloads on top of general-purpose operating systems, Deepcomet AI is building a system where AI is the core.
- **First-Class Tensors**: No more clunky library wrappers. Neural networks are treated as first-class citizens.
- **Memory Safety**: Compile-time guarantees without a garbage collector.
- **Direct NPU Targeting**: Bypass CPU bottlenecks entirely with MLIR lowering.
